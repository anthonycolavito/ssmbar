About this dashboard. Benefits are computed under current law per an age 65 claim age using SSA's 2025 Trustees Report intermediate assumptions. Real values are expressed in 2026 dollars using the Trustees' GDP price index. Cohort unisex life expectancy at age 65 is the simple average of male and female cohort life expectancy at 65 from the Trustees Report. Earnings profiles follow SSA scaled-earner conventions (very low, low, medium, high, max). Couples are assumed to share birth years and claim ages. Each metric is shown under both the scheduled scenario (the formula-based benefit current law promises) and the payable scenario (the share of scheduled benefits the OASDI trust fund can actually finance after its projected depletion).
